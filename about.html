Meet Aldras



Our mission and why we do what we do

Inspiration

We're passionate about accelerating progress and allowing valuable human capital to be focused on responsibilities that add the most value.


Technology has progressed so far that many monotonous manual tasks like manufacturing or even cleaning clothes no longer require continuos human effort.


Technological evolution can also help accelerate modern digital workflows, just as it has with physical tasks. We seek to close gaps where effort is still being spent on repetitive computer tasks.

History

The concept behind Aldras started as the ideas of a few engineering students during their internship and research experiences. Little by little, specialized scripts were coded to advance repetitive tasks like standard work documentation or data processing.

Coworkers began to ask for scripts of their own to speed up their own workflows. Eventually, there was the breakthrough to generalize the automation scripting to allow it to be customized to any process.

As this all-purpose computer automation tool developed, so did petitions for its benefits and the potential to help other individuals and organizations became apparent.

Applications

We at Aldras believe all individuals and organizations have an obligation to better the world around us.

We are very proud of our charitable organization and COVID-19 programs that provide the Aldras automation suite at or below cost to those working hardest to help others.
