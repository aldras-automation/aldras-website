Frequently asked questions

How does the free trial work?

The free trial allows access to the Professional plan for 30 days with no credit card required. Simply enter your email address to receive your trial key, download Aldras, and enter the trial key to activate.

Do you offer any discounts?

Yes! Our mission includes additional effort to facilitate the work of students, nonprofits, and those fighting COVID-19. See more about those specialty programs above. Plans with multiple users also come with discounts.

Can I change my plan?

Yes, you can change or cancel your plan at any time and scale up or down as you desire. Billing takes place at the beginning of the service period.

How do I access support?

All plans can utilize the Docs and send emails to the Aldras team. Tickets from plans with priority support are addressed first and also have access to phone support and consultation.

What can I automate?

You can program nearly anything that you as a human do to interact with your computer. This includes mouse movements and clicks, keyboard strokes, hotkeys, pauses, and more!

What can I not automate?

Unfortunately, due to the unique implementations in different operating systems, programs, and devices, scrolling is very inconsistent and has therefore been removed. See the Docs for recommendations.

Is Aldras for me?

Aldras is for individuals or organizations that would like to accelerate straightforward repetitive computer tasks to concentrate their own time and effort on more valuable responsibilities. We have seen applications of Aldras in the areas of engineering, customer success, sales, paperwork, data processing, and much more.

Aldras is about delivering value for people. Even so, we know that Aldras is not for everyone. From a simple return on investment perspective, if we assume your time is valued at $10 per hour and you have less than one hour of repetitive computer work every month, Aldras is likely not the best fit for you at this time. Additionally, if your team has direct access to the developers of the programs that you use in your workflows, you may have already worked with them to streamline your processes.
