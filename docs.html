Installation

Step 1

Download the Windows installer here.

Step 2

Run the installer. You may see some warnings during the beta testing pilot program. Click for more info, then continue and complete the installation with your desired options.

Step 3

Launch Aldras for the first time. You will be prompted to select a default folder where you would like to save your workflow automation files.

That's it! You're all set to start automating your workflows! See the Getting Started section for more.

Getting Started

Your First Automation: Hello World

Step 1

Enter a name for your workflow and click 'OK'.


Step 2

This will open the Edit window. Click 'Record' and configure your options to prime a recording session. Press the right CTRL key three times to start recording your actions.


Step 3

Press the left CTRL key once to record the position of your cursor without a click. Then move your mouse to a different position and press the left CTRL key again. Repeat a few times as you desire.


Step 4

Press right CTRL key three times again to stop recording and click 'Finish'.


Step 5

This will return you to the Edit window where you can see the actions you just recorded. Click 'Execute' and configure your options to prime the workflow for execution. Press the right CTRL key three times to start executing your commands.


Step 6

Adapt and change as you desire! All the actions you recorded are editable and you can add new commands with the '+' button.


Bonus Tip

The mouse monitor tool is located under Tools > Mouse Monitor in the menu bar and can help you identify mouse coordinates without recording.
