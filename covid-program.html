COVID-19 Program



Focus on combatting COVID-19 and saving lives

This era is one that will live in our memories forever. We are heartened by the continued fight of people putting their lives at risk to help others. The COVID program facilitates access to Aldras for organizations working incredibly hard to battle this pandemic or those strongly affected by COVID-19.

Application



Manage the license keys in your organization.

Individual usage plans distribute license keys for each user to use without limits.
Floating usage allows for a pool of licenses to be shared within your organization.


The Aldras COVID program is a provisional program. Admittance is not guaranteed and Aldras reserves the right to revoke enrollment.
